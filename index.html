StarBull Whitepaper v1.0

Executive Summary

StarBull represents a paradigm shift in the meme token space by combining viral appeal with real utility, sustainable tokenomics, and community-driven governance. Our 23-stage presale model ensures fair distribution while our innovative tax structure funds ongoing development and marketing.

Technical Architecture

Built on the Binance Smart Chain for low transaction fees and high scalability, StarBull implements advanced smart contract features including automatic liquidity provision, reflection rewards, and a progressive tax system that benefits long-term holders.

Tokenomics Deep Dive

With a total supply of 1 billion tokens and a carefully designed distribution model, StarBull ensures sustainable growth. The 0% buy tax encourages adoption while the 5% sell tax (1% burn, 2% marketing, 2% liquidity) creates a deflationary mechanism and funds ecosystem development.

Security & Transparency

Security is our top priority. The StarBull contract has been audited by leading blockchain security firms, liquidity is locked for 24 months, and ownership will be renounced post-launch to ensure complete decentralization and community control.
